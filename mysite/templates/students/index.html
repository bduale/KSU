Overview

Khaatumo State University is located in Somalia. It is a world-class higher education institution that offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in many fields - Islamic Studies, Education, Business Management, Mathematics, Science, Computer Science, Engineering, Health Sciences, and important languages - Somali, Arabic, Turkish, and English.
